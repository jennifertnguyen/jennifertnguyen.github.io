  • About Me

    My name is Jennifer, I am a goal oriented and engaged professional driven to merge hard techniques and creative innovation delivering elevated end of the line communications. Currently, I am enrolled in the Business Administration program at the University of Texas at Dallas and will begin my Masters degree in December 2015.

    Presently, I work at Ashlar Projects writing and editing copy for clients such as Uptown Dallas, University Crossing, and a host of lifestyle brands. I also assist in proposal writing, naming studies, and advising on graphic design for our clientele. Previously, I worked at Poo~Pourri as the Marketing Intern where I developed my research and PR acumen.

    Please feel free to peruse my site where you can find pieces of my work, my updated resume, and my contact information.

    Ronald McDonald House

    For my marketing branding course, I was asked to select a non-profit organization in the DFW area – I choose to work with Ronald McDonald House Dallas – and assist the director with their North Texas Giving Day branding. North Texas Giving Day is an all day event in the North Texas region, where non-profits seek funds from north Texans. My task was to analyze their brand, develop a marketing goal for the event, create and execute on said plan, and lastly, follow up with analytics.

    Net 0 Building Proposal

    For my Business Communications class, this presentation required a team of four to research, develop, and propose a green energy plan for the University of Texas at Dallas. To make the case for a Net 0 Building, we conducted ample amount of outside research to develop a thorough business proposal and presentation. Upon presenting our plan at the UTD Green Fair, our group’s outline was voted as “The Best Business Plan” by the Fair’s judges.
